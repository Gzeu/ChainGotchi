## FAQ â“

**Q: Can I revive a dead pet?**
A: No, death is permanent. This creates real stakes and engagement.

**Q: Can I sell my pet?**
A: Yes! Pets are ERC721 NFTs and can be traded on marketplaces.

**Q: What happens if I miss a day?**
A: Your pet's hunger will decrease. If it reaches 0, your pet dies permanently.

**Q: Can I rename my pet?**
A: No, names are permanent and stored on-chain.

**Q: How do I know my pet's current hunger?**
A: Check the pet card in the app. Hunger updates in real-time.

**Q: Can I battle the same opponent multiple times?**
A: Yes, but each pet has a 1-hour battle cooldown.

**Q: What's the fastest way to level up?**
A: Combine regular playing (hourly) with battle wins (100 XP each).
